The Problem

Mental Health Support Is Broken

In Saudi Arabia, a single therapy session costs 300-800 SAR. 70% of people needing help hesitate due to social stigma. Wait times are 2-4 weeks. And smaller cities have almost no mental health specialists. Al-Ma'ali addresses all four barriers at once.

The Solution

Your AI Therapist — Free, Private, Always On

Al-Ma'ali provides AI-powered mental health conversations with full encryption and anonymity. Free 24/7 chat, clinical psychological assessments, progress tracking, and smart referrals to real specialists when needed.

Key Features

How It Works

Privacy First

Anonymous Registration

Sign up with a nickname — or no name at all. Full end-to-end encryption. No data sharing, no ads. Everything can be permanently deleted at any time. Privacy is the foundation, not a feature.

Home

Smart Dashboard

Daily wellness tip, streak tracking, and 4 smart services at your fingertips: start a new journey (assessment), AI confidential chat, check your progress, and performance reports.

Assessment

Psychological Assessment

Clinical-grade screening using PHQ-9 and GAD-7 scales. Scores from 0-100 determine your level. Severe cases get immediate referral to emergency services. Retake anytime to track your progress.

AI Therapy

Confidential AI Chat

Talk to an AI therapist 24/7. It listens, asks questions, gives practical advice, and tracks recurring themes. No judgment, no stigma. Encrypted conversations that only you can see.

Analytics

Performance Analytics

Compare your initial assessment with your current state. See your improvement percentage. The example shows a jump from 45 to 88 — a 95.6% improvement. Data-driven proof that the journey is working.

Reports

Smart Report & Specialist Referral

AI-generated mental health report analyzing your condition with actionable recommendations. If the app isn't enough, it refers you to nearby partner hospitals and clinics matched to your specific needs, insurance, and budget.

Business Model

Freemium + Hospital Partnerships

Free unlimited AI chat for everyone. Premium monthly reports (29-49 SAR/month) with detailed analytics, clinical assessments, and specialist referrals through partner hospitals. A sustainable model that keeps the core free.
